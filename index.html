Welcome to Elfinwood Jade

Most of the Greenstone on this site has been collected from local beaches over roughly 25 years by myself. Having a passion for this beautiful stone led me to learning how to enhance these beach finds into unique jewellery. After learning how to polish a stone by hand I learnt how to carve, this has been a long slow journey. Every stone teaches me something new and every stone has its own unique character.

Welcome to Elfinwood Jade

Most of the Greenstone on this site has been collected from local beaches over roughly 25 years by myself. Having a passion for this beautiful stone led me to learn how to how to polish a stone by hand. After this I completed a couple of evening courses at the polytechnic on how to carve.

For me this hobby has been an ever evolving process over many years, I now have a small carving studio at home where I can continue to learn how to enhance these beautiful beach finds and turn them into unique jewellery.

Every stone teaches me something new and every stone has its own unique character.
